How the AI works

01
Feature extraction
Each violation record is encoded as [latitude, longitude, hour, impact_score]. StandardScaler normalises all four dimensions before clustering.
02
K-Means clustering
scikit-learn KMeans (K=8, n_init=10) groups violations into 8 spatiotemporal zones. K was chosen by elbow method on inertia vs. cluster count.
03
Risk scoring
Each cluster is scored by total impact (sum of violation × junction weights). Clusters ranked Critical → Low based on proportional impact share.
04
Production path
Live CCTV frames → YOLOv8 detects illegal parking → classified violation written to DB → cluster model updates enforcement zones in near real-time.
